open source / good software alts

picture editing

for picture editing use either gimp, inkscape or paint.net instead of photoshop.

browser

avoid chrome, edge, opera and operagx. especially opera, as a chinese company owns them. use librewolf or firefox w/ a privacy profile.

search engine

i would shill duckduckgo here, but they censor searches and also show bing results. use searx, searxng or whoogle.

vpns

popular vpns like nordvpn and expressvpn most likely collect data and are prone to data breaches. use either i2p or tor, or for a conventional vpn use mullard.
